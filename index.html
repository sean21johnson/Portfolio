About Me

I have always been fascinated by the web, but never put the time in to better understand the mechanics behind it. After feeling a lack of fulfillment in various financial operations roles, I decided to explore options in web development. After a few modules in Thinkful's prep course, I became increasingly interested in enrolling in a full-time bootcamp and making a career change. I have never been more excited about the future of my career and cannot wait to begin learning from and collaborating with others in the software engineering space.

When I'm not coding, I am usually exercising, reading or watching sports. My favorite exercise is running and I typically run at least fifteen miles a week. I mostly read nonfiction biographies about successful entrepreneurs, and also enjoy reading interesting business articles online. My favorite sports teams are the New York Jets and the Brooklyn Nets. Before Covid, I got to the Barclay's Center and Meadowlands Stadium for at least one game every season.
